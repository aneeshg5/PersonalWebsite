## Projects and Research

Aneesh's portfolio spans several areas. On the systems side, OrbitForge is a browser-based satellite orbit simulator (C++17 compiled to WebAssembly) that runs three Kalman filters concurrently — KF, EKF, and a 12-state Multiplicative Extended Kalman Filter — against real TLE orbital data, with Monte Carlo consistency validation and a custom WebGL2 renderer. LeafScan AI is a full-stack mobile app (React Native + FastAPI + PyTorch) for plant disease diagnosis with Mahalanobis-distance out-of-distribution detection, a keyword-gated RAG agronomist chat, and autonomous drone scan integration. Cryogenic Flow Classifier is a NASA Artemis HLS program project using ML to classify cryogenic flow regimes in real time. ZenGM Sports is a full-stack sports management simulation platform. Research includes a battery degradation prediction pipeline (PyTorch, MLflow, Optuna), Josephson junction quantum hardware research, and CAN bus embedded security work.
